Stress is a common experience in daily life, especially during academic, professional, or personal challenges. Learning how to manage stress effectively is important for maintaining both mental and physical well-being.

1. Practice mindfulness or meditation to stay calm and focused.
2. Take short breaks during work or study sessions to avoid burnout.
3. Stay organized by setting realistic goals and priorities.
4. Drink enough water and maintain healthy eating habits.
5. Limit screen time and social media usage to reduce mental fatigue.
6. Share your feelings with trusted friends, family members, or counselors.

Following these simple habits regularly can help you stay relaxed, improve concentration, and build emotional resilience.
